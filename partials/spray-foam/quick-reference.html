• Board Feet (BF) = Sq Ft × Thickness (in).
  • Roof Sq Ft = Length × Width × Pitch Multiplier.
  • Gable Sq Ft = 0.5 × Base × Height × Count.
  • Linear Sq Ft = LF × Band Height (ft).
  • Adjusted BF = Raw BF × (1 + framing factor) × (1 + waste factor).
  • Sell Price = Total Cost / (1 - target margin).
